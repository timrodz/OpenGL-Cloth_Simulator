Cloth Simulator

F1: Drops the cloth on the floor
F2: Decreases the width
F3: Increases the width
F4: Decreases the length
F5: Increases the length
F6: Turns on the fan
F7: Decreases the fan's speed
F8: Increases the fan's speed

Press R to reset the scene.

Mouse Click and drag the cloth, while pressing down the left mouse button.
To tear the cloth and create holes, just click on the cloth.

Clicking and drag the other shapes on the floor, while pressing down the left mouse button.

WASD to move the camera
